## MAG_CAL=7 Effectiveness — See [topic](../topics/mag_cal_7_verification.md)

Summary: max yaw divergence 5.3 degrees (vs 24 degrees in log201 with MAG_CAL=4).
No yaw inconsistency error. All MAG_FUSION state transitions verified correct.

## Aggressive Takeoff — See [topic](../topics/outdoor_tuning_sfdv1.md)

Summary: 0.92m altitude overshoot, 139 cm/s peak climb rate. Root cause is
MOT_THST_HOVER=0.125 vs actual hover ThO=0.069 (80% feedforward error).
Actual acceleration was 5.5x desired (13.8 vs 2.5 m/s^2).

## Roll Oscillation — See [topic](../topics/outdoor_tuning_sfdv1.md)

Summary: 8-10 Hz limit cycle in angle-rate cascade. Pure tuning issue (no physical
noise contribution). Caused by high autotune gains + asymmetric airframe (wider
lateral motor spacing, lower roll inertia). Roll overshoot ratio 1.52 vs pitch 1.38.
